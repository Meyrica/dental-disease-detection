ILUSTRATION OF DENTAL DISEASE

Here are some examples of dental diseases that can be detected by the system.

Gingivitis

Inflammation of the gums characterized by redness, swelling and easy bleeding.

Plaque

A sticky layer containing bacteria that sticks to the surface of the teeth and can cause tooth decay.

Healthy

Condition of teeth and gums that are healthy, clean, and free from signs of disease.

Calculus

Dental plaque hardens due to the accumulation of minerals over a long period of time.

Caries

Damage to hard tooth tissue caused by the demineralization of enamel by bacterial acid
